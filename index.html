🏦 Bank Talk Toolkit — Interactive Lessons

Pagamento incorreto ao beneficiário no exterior

Casual: “We sent the payment to the overseas beneficiary with incorrect details.”
(Enviamos o pagamento ao beneficiário no exterior com detalhes incorretos.)
Formal: “An incorrect international payment to the beneficiary abroad was identified and is under review for correction.”
(Foi identificado um pagamento internacional incorreto ao beneficiário no exterior e está sendo revisado para correção.)
🧠 Key terms: payment, beneficiary, international payment.
🚫 Avoid Saying / Evite Dizer: ✖ “We did a wrong payment.”
✔ Instead say: “We made an incorrect international payment.”

Pagamento duplicado ao beneficiário no exterior

Casual: “Looks like the overseas beneficiary got paid twice.”
(Parece que o beneficiário no exterior recebeu o pagamento duplicado.)
Formal: “A duplicate payment to the overseas beneficiary was identified due to a processing error, and a recall request has been initiated.”
(Foi identificado um pagamento duplicado ao beneficiário no exterior devido a um erro de processamento, e um pedido de estorno foi iniciado.)
🧠 Key terms: duplicate payment, beneficiary, recall request.
🚫 Avoid Saying / Evite Dizer: ✖ “We did a double payment.”
✔ Instead say: “We made a duplicate payment.”

Recolhimento incorreto de impostos

Casual: “We’ve got an issue with the tax withholding — it was processed incorrectly.”
(Temos um problema com o recolhimento de impostos — foi feito incorretamente.)
Formal: “An incorrect tax withholding was identified. The transaction is under review to determine the cause and impact.”
(Foi identificado um recolhimento incorreto de impostos. A transação está sendo analisada para apurar a causa e o impacto.)
🧠 Key terms: tax withholding, incorrect tax withholding, tax collection.
🚫 Avoid Saying / Evite Dizer: ✖ “Wrong collect of taxes.”
✔ Instead say: “Incorrect tax withholding.”

Incidente de queda do sistema de câmbio

Casual: “The FX system went down this morning — trades couldn’t be processed.”
(O sistema de câmbio caiu esta manhã — não foi possível processar as operações.)
Formal: “We experienced an FX system outage that temporarily impacted currency trading operations.”
(Houve uma interrupção no sistema de câmbio que afetou temporariamente as operações de moeda.)
🧠 Key terms: FX system, FX system outage, currency trading operations, trades.
🚫 Avoid Saying / Evite Dizer: ✖ “The exchange system fell.”
✔ Instead say: “FX system outage” or “Foreign exchange system downtime.”

Sistema do Banco Central fora do ar

Casual: “The Central Bank system’s down again — payments are on hold.”
(O sistema do Banco Central caiu de novo — os pagamentos estão parados.)
Formal: “The Central Bank platform is currently unavailable, affecting settlement and payment processing.”
(A plataforma do Banco Central está indisponível no momento, afetando a liquidação e o processamento de pagamentos.)
🧠 Key terms: Central Bank system, Central Bank platform, payments, settlement.
🚫 Avoid Saying / Evite Dizer: ✖ “The Central Bank system stopped.”
✔ Instead say: “Central Bank system is down.”

Lentidão no sistema Citrix

Casual: “Citrix is running super slow today — can’t even open the dashboard.”
(O Citrix está super lento hoje — nem consigo abrir o painel.)
Formal: “We’re experiencing performance issues on the Citrix platform impacting user access.”
(Estamos enfrentando lentidão na plataforma Citrix que afeta o acesso dos usuários.)
🧠 Key terms: Citrix, performance issues, dashboard, user access.
🚫 Avoid Saying / Evite Dizer: ✖ “Citrix is very slow today.”
✔ Instead say: “Citrix system slowness” or “Performance degradation on Citrix.”

Pedido de demissão de colaborador

Casual: “John handed in his resignation today.”
(John pediu demissão hoje.)
Formal: “We received a formal resignation notice from the employee.”
(Recebemos o pedido formal de demissão do colaborador.)
🧠 Key terms: handed in his resignation, resignation notice.
🚫 Avoid Saying / Evite Dizer: ✖ “He gave his demission.”
✔ Instead say: “Employee resignation.”

Abertura de processo de contratação junto ao time de Paris

Casual: “We’re kicking off a hiring process with the Paris team.”
(Estamos iniciando um processo de contratação com o time de Paris.)
Formal: “The recruitment process has been initiated in coordination with the Paris office.”
(O processo de contratação foi iniciado em coordenação com o escritório de Paris.)
🧠 Key terms: kicking off a hiring process, recruitment process.
🚫 Avoid Saying / Evite Dizer: ✖ “We are opening a process of hiring.”
✔ Instead say: “Starting the recruitment process with our Paris office.”

Processo interno para escalar perdas operacionais

Casual: “We need to escalate this loss through the proper internal process.”
(Precisamos escalar essa perda pelo processo interno correto.)
Formal: “An internal escalation process has been initiated to assess and record the operational loss.”
(Foi iniciado um processo interno de escalonamento para avaliar e registrar a perda operacional.)
🧠 Key terms: escalate, internal process, operational loss.
🚫 Avoid Saying / Evite Dizer: ✖ “We are growing the problem.”
✔ Instead say: “We are escalating the issue internally.”
